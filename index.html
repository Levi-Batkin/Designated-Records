About Us

What we stand for

Promotional Based Label

At Designated, we prioritize artist promotion by covering all costs ourselves, ensuring our artists can focus on their craft without financial burdens. All artwork will be 100% original and we will also share your song over social media for maximum exposure.

Simple Contracts Policy

We offer simple, no-strings-attached contracts. If you're happy with the release, you can even discuss signing onto our label.

The Most Affordable Deals

Every artist on the label will receive a generous 60% of all profits, as well as full freedom to pursue their goals. The label will even pay out profits that total less than £5.

Your Brand is Safe

Artists can rest easy knowing their brand is in safe hands with Designated Records. We guarantee brand safety by ensuring all legal documents are drafted by an expert lawyer.

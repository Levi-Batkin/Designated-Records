About Designated Records

A fresh, innovative music label with roots in the UK, combining genres with forward thinking into sound.

Designated Records LTD was founded in 2022 by talented multigenre producer Levi Batkin, combining a wide range of musical influences from different parts of the UK. Drawing inspiration from garage, drum and bass, bassline, UK hip hop, and drill, the label creates unexpected, fresh sonic experiences that redefine contemporary music.

We're on a mission to give the world's listeners new dimensions by which to perceive and understand music. Designated Records is not restricted to any genre or style; instead, we're committed to bringing forth multigenre tracks selected with care that challenge conventions in production.

This creative freedom allows us to produce songs that feel both innovative and accessible, capturing the essence of UK music culture while introducing fresh sounds to a global audience. At the centre of Designated Records lies a vision of collaboration, working with artists from many walks of life in different parts of the world.

By fostering talent and offering newcomers a moment to shine, we've created a positive music culture wherein both emerging and established musicians can grow and evolve. Since inception, Designated Records has grown to be well-reputed for its fresh approach and true dedication to artist development, making a meaningful impact in the global music scene.

Designated Records LTD is an innovative music label based in the UK, known for its unique blend of genres and forward-thinking approach to sound. Founded in 2022 by the talented multigenre producer Levi Batkin, the label brings together a diverse range of musical influences from across the UK. Drawing from genres such as garage, drum and bass, bassline, UK hip hop, and drill, the label crafts unexpected and fresh sonic experiences that redefine what it means to create contemporary music. The label's mission is to offer listeners a new way to engage with music. Designated Records LTD is not confined to one genre or style but is instead dedicated to delivering carefully curated, multigenre tracks that push the boundaries of musical production. This creative freedom allows the label to produce songs that feel both innovative and accessible, capturing the essence of UK music culture while introducing fresh sounds to a global audience. At the heart of Designated Records LTD is Levi Batkin's vision of collaboration. The label works with artists from various backgrounds and regions, encouraging musical exchange and collaboration. By fostering talent and providing a platform for new artists to shine, Batkin has nurtured a positive music culture that allows both emerging and established musicians to grow and evolve. Since its inception, Designated Records LTD has quickly gained recognition for its unique approach and its commitment to supporting the creative journeys of its artists, making a significant impact on the global music scene.

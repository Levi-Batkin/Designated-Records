About Us

What we stand for

Promotional Based Label

At Designated, we prioritize artist promotion by covering all costs ourselves, ensuring our artists can focus on their craft without financial burdens.

Simple Contracts Policy

We believe in artist empowerment, which is why at Designated, we use sensible contracts and ensure they retain 100% of their royalties after any required label distribution fees.

Distribution

At Designated, we ensure maximum exposure for our artists by distributing their songs to all major music platforms.


Promotions

We go the extra mile by providing mastering services and promotional content, including artwork, to our artists at no cost, supporting their artistic vision from start to finish.

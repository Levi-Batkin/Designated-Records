About Designated Records

Welcome to Designated Records

Designated Records LTD is a fresh, innovative music label with its roots in the UK, known for combining genres with forward thinking into sound.

The label was founded in 2022 by talented multigenre producer Levi Batkin and combines a wide range of musical influences from different parts of the UK.

Drawing inspiration from garage, drum and bass, bassline, UK hip hop, and drill, the label makes unexpected, fresh sonic experiences named for the reason behind its crafting of what it means to create contemporary music.

Designated Records LTD is on a mission to give the world's listeners new dimensions by which to perceive and understand music. Designated Records LTD is not restricted to any genre or style; instead, they are committed to bringing forth multigenre tracks selected with care and that challenge conventions in production.

This creative freedom allows the label to produce songs that feel both innovative and accessible, capturing the essence of UK music culture while introducing fresh sounds to a global audience.

At the centre of Designated Records LTD lies Levi Batkin's vision of collaboration. The label works with artists from many walks of life in different parts of the world, enabling musical exchange and collaboration.

Fostering talent and offering newbies a moment to shine, Batkin has created a positive music culture wherein both emerging and established musicians can grow and evolve.

Since its inception, Designated Records LTD has grown to be well-reputed for its fresh approach and true dedication to the growth of its artists' careers, making a dent in the music scene worldwide.
